lgh_profitcal: instructions

Purpose
- Calculate profit after selling a Swedish apartment, with clear inputs and computed outputs.

Inputs (default/test values)
- Forsaljningspris: 4 350 000
- Lan: 1 950 000
- Inkopspris: 380 000
- Maklararvode: 65 000
- Styling: 0
- Fotografering: 0
- Renoveringskostnader: 200 000
- Ovriga avdragsgilla kostnader: 0
- Kapitaltillskott: 10 000
- Skattesats (%): 22

Calculations
- Anskaffningskostnad / Totalt avdrag (calc1) = Inkopspris + Maklararvode + Styling + Fotografering + Renoveringskostnader + Ovriga avdragsgilla kostnader + Kapitaltillskott = 645 000 + 10 000 = 655 000 kr
- Vinst fore skatt (calc2) = Forsaljningspris - calc1 = 4 350 000 - 655 000 = 3 695 000 kr
- Skatt (calc3) = calc2 * Skattesats = 3 695 000 * 22% = 812 900 kr
- Nettoresultat efter skatt (calc4) = calc2 - calc3 = 3 695 000 - 812 900 = 2 882 100 kr
- Vinst (calc5) = calc4 - Lan = 2 882 100 - 1 950 000 = 932 100 kr

UI layout hints
- Title: "Resultat vid forsäljning av LGH" and "Berakningsdata" section.
- Inputs: labeled number fields; accept plain numbers; format as SEK (kr) on display.
- Outputs: show computed fields (calc1..calc5) with currency formatting and the chosen tax rate as percent.

Behavior
- Recompute live on input change.
- Clamp empty/invalid inputs to 0; clamp tax to 0-100.
- Keep calculations pure client-side (HTML + JS, no backend).
- Make the UI as compact as possible so it works comfortably on mobile.
